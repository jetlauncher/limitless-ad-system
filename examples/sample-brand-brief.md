# Sample Brand Brief

## Brand
Limitless Club

## Audience
Thai business owners, founders, executives, and marketers who want practical AI leverage, not technical complexity.

## Offer positioning
We teach business-first AI: how to use AI to save time, increase leverage, improve decision-making, create content faster, and build systems that support growth.

## Tone
- direct
- premium
- practical
- founder-to-founder
- no fluff

## Visual direction
- dark luxury base
- premium editorial photography
- gold or warm metallic accent
- clean typography
- confident, modern, business-first look

## Words to use
- leverage
- systems
- operators
- founders
- scale
- practical
- no-code
- real business use

## Words to avoid
- geeky jargon
- childish AI hype
- futuristic sci-fi clichés
- robotic corporate language

## CTA style
- ดูรายละเอียด
- ลงชื่อดูรอบถัดไป
- ขอรายละเอียดเวิร์กช็อป
- ทักเพื่อรับข้อมูลเพิ่ม
